## 💡 How to Use

### **Adding Transactions**
1. **Select transaction type** - Income or Expense
2. **Enter amount** in rupees
3. **Add description** - What was this for?
4. **Choose party** - Who you paid to/received from
5. **Select payment method** - Cash, GPay, Online, etc.
6. **Pick category** - Categories change based on income/expense
7. **Set date** - Defaults to today
8. **Add notes** (optional) - Additional details
9. **Click "Save Transaction"**

### **Viewing & Filtering**
1. **Switch to "View & Filter" tab**
2. **Use filters** to narrow down transactions:
   - **Type**: Income only, Expense only, or both
   - **Payment Method**: Filter by specific methods
   - **Party**: Search by name
   - **Date Range**: From/To dates
   - **Category**: Filter by categories
3. **Click "Apply Filters"** to see results
4. **Export to CSV** for Excel analysis

### **Generating Reports**
1. **Switch to "Reports" tab**
2. **Select report period**:
   - This Month, Last Month
   - This Year, Last Year
   - All Time, Custom Range
3. **View comprehensive analytics**:
   - Income/Expense summaries
   - Net balance and savings rate
   - Top categories and payment methods
   - Monthly trend chart

### **Managing Transactions**
- **Edit**: Click ✏️ button next to any transaction
- **Delete**: Click 🗑️ button (with confirmation)
- **Search**: Use party filter to find specific transactions

## 📊 Understanding Reports

### **Balance Summary (Header)**
- **Total Income**: All-time income total
- **Total Expenses**: All-time expense total  
- **Net Balance**: Income minus expenses
- **This Month**: Current month's net balance

### **Report Cards**
- **Income Summary**: Total income, transaction count, top source
- **Expense Summary**: Total expenses, transaction count, top category
- **Net Balance**: Net amount, status, savings rate percentage
- **Payment Methods**: Breakdown by payment method usage

### **Monthly Trend Chart**
- **Green bars**: Income by month
- **Red bars**: Expenses by month
- **Visual comparison** of income vs expenses over time
